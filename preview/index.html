The Science

Ingredients that earn their place

Every active is chosen for clinical evidence, not trend. Here's what's inside — and why.

💧
Hyaluronic Acid
Deep hydration

Holds up to 1,000× its weight in water. Multi-weight formula for surface and deeper skin layers.

🌿
Niacinamide
Pore refinement & tone

Regulates sebum, minimises pores, and fades pigmentation at 10% concentration.

🔬
Peptides
Firmness support

Signal proteins that encourage collagen synthesis. Non-irritating age support.

✨
Vitamin C
Brightening & antioxidant

Stabilised ascorbic acid at 15% for even tone and daily environmental defence.

🛡️
Ceramides
Barrier restoration

Lipids identical to those in healthy skin. Seals moisture and strengthens the skin barrier.

🌱
Centella Asiatica
Calming & healing

Madecassoside and asiaticoside reduce redness and support wound healing.

🌙
Retinol Alternative
Cell renewal

Bakuchiol — plant-derived retinol analogue. Comparable efficacy, suitable for sensitive skin.

🍵
Green Tea
Antioxidant defence

EGCG-rich extract neutralises free radicals and soothes inflammation.
