Questions

Common questions.

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day return on all unopened products in their original packaging. If a product has caused an unexpected skin reaction, please contact us directly — we'll work with you individually. To start a return, email hello@sebble.com with your order number.

  • How long does delivery take?

    Standard UK delivery takes 2–3 business days. Express next-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2pm Monday–Friday. International delivery to EU countries typically takes 4–6 business days.

  • Can I get personalised skincare advice?

    Yes — and it's free. Mention in your message that you'd like skincare advice, along with your skin type, primary concern, and any products you're currently using. One of our skin specialists will respond with a tailored routine recommendation.

  • Are your products suitable during pregnancy?

    Most are, with one exception: our Retinal Bio-Lift Night Serum contains an encapsulated retinoid (Retinaldehyde), which is best avoided during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Our Glow + Marks, Peptide Repair Barrier, Caviar Lift (retinol-free), Exosome PDRN Repair, and Neuro Peptide Eye serums are all considered pregnancy-friendly. As always, we recommend checking with your healthcare provider before introducing new products.

  • Do you offer wholesale or partnership enquiries?

    Yes — we work with select independent retailers, spas, and clinical practices. Contact us at hello@sebble.com with the subject line 'Partnership' and we'll be in touch within three business days.
