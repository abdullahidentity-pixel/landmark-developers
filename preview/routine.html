A great skincare routine doesn't need to be complicated. It needs to be consistent, well-ordered, and matched to your skin.

Morning

Morning Routine

  • 1

    Facial Spray (optional)

    Mist lightly on a clean face to prepare the skin surface and enhance absorption of the next step.

  • 2

    Serum

    Apply 3–5 drops and press gently into skin. Allow 60 seconds to absorb before the next step.

  • 3

    Barrier Cream

    Apply a pea-sized amount to seal in your serum and lock in moisture. Suitable for all skin types.

  • 4

    SPF 30+

    Non-negotiable when using active ingredients. Apply generously as the absolute final morning step.

Evening

Evening Routine

  • 1

    Double Cleanse

    Start with an oil cleanser to dissolve sunscreen and makeup. Follow with a gentle water-based cleanser.

  • 2

    Serum

    Apply your PM serum. Keep the Retinal Bio-Lift Night Serum to evenings only, and don't layer it with the Glow + Marks Vitamin C in the same session.

  • 3

    Barrier Cream

    The most important evening step. Seals in active ingredients while your skin's repair mechanisms are active.

Common pitfalls

Mistakes to avoid when building your routine.

01

Layering incompatible actives

Mixing AHAs/BHAs with Retinol in the same routine causes significant irritation. Use them on alternate evenings — they'll both work better for it.

02

Skipping SPF with actives

Vitamin C, AHAs, BHAs, and Retinol all increase photosensitivity. Without SPF, you're undoing the benefits. SPF 30 minimum, every morning.

03

Too many new products at once

If your skin reacts, you'll have no way to identify the cause. Introduce one new product per two-week period to accurately assess tolerance.

04

Not waiting for absorption

Applying moisturiser immediately after serum dilutes the active ingredients. Give each product 60–90 seconds to penetrate before applying the next.

05

Over-exfoliating

Using acids or Retinol every night breaks down the skin barrier and triggers inflammation. Every other night is the correct frequency, especially initially.

06

Abandoning the routine too early

Meaningful skin changes take 6–12 weeks of consistent use. Switching before this window closes means you'll never know what was actually working.
