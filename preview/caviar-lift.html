About this Serum

Caviar Lift is our premium flagship — the luxury choice for skin 35+. Instant-tightening polymers such as Pullulan create a visible 30-minute smoothing, lifted, plumped finish, while a peptide blend, Caviar Extract, Marine DNA, and Microalgae Extract support firmer, well-conditioned-looking skin over time.

A silky, cushioning texture layers beautifully under makeup and melts into skin without heaviness. Niacinamide, Glycerin, multi-weight Hyaluronic Acid, Ceramides, and Panthenol keep skin comfortable and hydrated through the day.

This formula is retinol-free, so it is gentle enough for daytime, special occasions, and nightly use alike. Best suited to normal, dry, and combination skin; if your skin is very sensitive, patch test first or speak with the Skin Consultant.

Questions

FAQ

How do I layer this with other serums?

Apply lighter, water-based serums first, followed by thicker textures. If using multiple Sebble serums, allow 60 seconds between each for full absorption before layering the next.

Can I use this morning and evening?

Yes — this formula is designed for AM and PM use. Always finish your morning routine with a broad-spectrum SPF 30+ to protect against UV exposure.

Is this suitable for sensitive skin?

Caviar Lift is retinol-free and generally well tolerated, but it is best suited to normal, dry, and combination skin. If your skin is very sensitive or reactive, we suggest starting with our Peptide Repair Barrier serum, or asking the Skin Consultant first. Always patch test for 24 hours before full facial use.

Is this safe to use during pregnancy?

We recommend consulting your healthcare provider or midwife before introducing new products during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.

How should I patch test?

Apply a small amount to the inner forearm or behind the ear. Leave unwashed for 24 hours. If no reaction occurs, proceed with regular facial use.

How long will a 30ml bottle last?

Using 2–3 drops twice daily, a 30ml bottle typically lasts 6–8 weeks with consistent use.
