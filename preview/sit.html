Trust & safety

Guidance you can trust, with clear limits.

SIT is a cosmetic skincare advisor — designed to help you choose products and build a routine. It is never a substitute for professional medical care.

Not a medical diagnosis

SIT offers cosmetic guidance and product recommendations only. It does not diagnose, treat, or claim to cure any skin condition.

Dermatologist referral

If you describe symptoms that may be painful, severe, or medically concerning, SIT pauses recommendations and suggests professional care.

Privacy-first photos

Photos are analysed to generate guidance and are never stored without your explicit consent. You can remove your image at any time.

Important: SIT provides general skincare guidance and product recommendations. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace advice from a qualified dermatologist or medical professional. If your skin concern is painful, severe, persistent, rapidly changing, infected, bleeding, or medically concerning, please consult a dermatologist.
